Research Interests

My research aims at integrating mathematical theories in control, communication, computing, and networking with emphasis on applications in biological/neurological/sociological networks and cyber-physical systems. I am currently working on the following topics:

  • Control over communication networks

  • Resilient control of cyber-physical systems

  • Network synchronization and control
