Research Interests

My research focuses on a systems-theoretic approach to controlling and designing complex networks with layered architectures. I am currently working on the following topics:

  • Safe and secure cyber-physical systems

  • Synchronization in large-scale brain networks

  • Robustness and efficiency of biological circuits
